The Perfect Sweet Potato

Description

A sweet and savory sweet potato.

Ingredients

  • A sweet potato
  • 1 tablespoon Kerrygold Butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon of red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Properly rinse sweet potato.
  3. Pat dry
  4. Place sweet potato on baking sheet
  5. Bake for 45mins - 1 hour
  6. Gently poke holes in sweet potato after 30mins of baking
  7. Once potato is fully baked, remove from oven and let cool for five minutes.
  8. Add butter and seasonings.
  9. Let cool for another five minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
